Bio

Fleetwood Mac are a British-American rock band, formed in London in 1967. Fleetwood Mac were founded
by guitarist Peter Green, drummer Mick Fleetwood and guitarist Jeremy Spencer, before bassist John McVie
joined the line-up for their self-titled debut album. Danny Kirwan joined as a third guitarist in 1968.
